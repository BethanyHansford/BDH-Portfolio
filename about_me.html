About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! I'm a London-based graphic designer with a passion for handcrafted, illustrative designs specializing in packaging and branding. With a record including a competition win and a first-class honours in graphic design at NUA (Norwich University of the Arts), I bring a diverse range of skills to my projects.

I specialize in creating visually captivating packaging that engages the senses and tells a unique story. By combining traditional handcrafting techniques with contemporary aesthetics, I ensure that every detail is carefully considered, resulting in striking and cohesive designs. Additionally, my love for hand-drawn illustrations adds a personal and authentic touch to my work, whether it's custom elements, patterns, or expressive typography.
